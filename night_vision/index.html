Night Vision

Smooth your viewing experience with intelligent real-time flash reduction and adaptive screen dimming.

🎯

Smart Localized Dimming

Advanced 8x8 spatial analysis identifies "hotspots" in real-time. Only the bright parts of the screen are damped, leaving dark areas perfectly readable.

🌊

Smoothing Filters

Configurable rolling buffers (5-30 frames) eliminate jitter and ensure fluid transitions, even at extreme 180 FPS capture rates.

🚀

Pro Performance

Bilinear-interpolated shadow maps provide a natural, soft-edged smoothing effect while keeping CPU and battery usage at a minimum.

1. Install normally

  • Open the downloaded NightVision.dmg
  • Drag the Night Vision.app icon into the Applications folder shortcut.
  • Double-click the app in your Applications folder to launch.

⚠️ "App is damaged" Error

Because this is a free app without a paid Apple Developer signature, Gatekeeper will block it. Fix it in seconds:

Option A: Terminal (Recommended) xattr -cr /Applications/Night\ Vision.app Option B: Right-Click

Right-Click (or Control-Click) the app and select Open.

Note: Night Vision requires Screen Recording permissions in System Settings to detect screen brightness.
