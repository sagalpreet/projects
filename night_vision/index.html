Flash Smoothing

Instantly detects rapid spikes in brightness and applies a momentary smoothing filter to protect your eyes from harsh strobe effects in videos.

🌙

Adaptive Dark Mode

Intelligently dims the screen when viewing bright white content (like spreadsheets or empty browser pages) to reduce glare without affecting clarity.

🔒

Privacy First

All screen analysis happens entirely on your device using ScreenCaptureKit. No data is ever recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Easy Installation

Download the DMG, drag it to your Applications folder, and grant Screen Recording permissions in System Settings to allow the app to detect flashes.
